Summary
Genesis10 is currently seeking a Data Engineer for a Remote position with a Financial Services Organization located in Minneapolis, MN. This is a 6+ month contract-to-hire opportunity.

Compensation: $55.00 - 70.00 per hour, W2.

This role is for a data engineer on the client's Ingestion Team, requiring at least 5 years of hands-on development and design experience with Spark, Databricks, and DBT. The ideal candidate will be a self-starter with strong skills in advanced SQL and Python, capable of taking initiative and leading their own work.

Responsibilities:
Apply expert industry knowledge to drive outcomes for customers
Work and problem solve independently on initiatives that align to the broader software engineering strategy
Design systems and software integration patterns across a diverse software engineering ecosystem
Use independent, critical thinking to solve complex problems which are significant to the customer
Develop the most architecturally impactful components of solutions
Lead efforts to identify, incorporate, and define development frameworks and libraries
Seek out industry and technology knowledge along with best practices to share with the team
Coordinate design and integration of the entire system including subsystems
Participate in team's collaboration sessions to provide technical expertise
Contribute to detailed application specifications, standards, and diagrams
Broker solution design and implementation across product teams to achieve outcomes
Lead the resolution of critical incidents and proactively address product issues
Promote and expand on the use of the CI/CD pipeline to improve the deployment and build process

Requirements:
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or other technical field or equivalent work experience
5+ years of experience in engineering environments, taking abstract concepts and ideas and formulating a detailed software engineering plan to deliver
Extensive hands-on experience (5+ years) with technologies such as Apache Spark, Databricks, DBT, Python, and Airflow
5+ years of experience designing and developing reusable frameworks to streamline and standardize data engineering processes
Experience architecting software solutions with requirements such as performance SLOs, high availability, reliability, security, etc
Understand data and system integration patterns and technologies
Strong conceptual, critical thinking, technical and problem-solving skills with good attention to details

Desired skills:
Financial services industry experience

Only candidates available and ready to work directly as Genesis10 employees will be considered for this position.
