Summary
Optum is a global organization that delivers care, aided by technology to help millions of people live healthier lives. The work you do with our team will directly improve health outcomes by connecting people with the care, pharmacy benefits, data and resources they need to feel their best.Here, you will find a culture guided by inclusion, talented peers, comprehensive benefits and career development opportunities. Come make an impact on the communities we serve as you help us advance health optimization on a global scale.Join us to start Caring. Connecting. Growing together.

You will enjoy the flexibility to telecommute* from anywhere within the U.S. as you take on some tough challenges.

Primary Responsibilities:

Design, develop, test, and maintain high-quality software solutions aligned to business and technical requirements
Contribute to application architecture and technical design decisions for scalable, reliable systems
Build and enhance APIs, services, and user interfaces using modern frameworks and engineering best practices
Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code with a focus on performance and security
Participate in code reviews to ensure quality, consistency, and adherence to standards
Collaborate with product managers, architects, designers, and cross-functional teams to deliver features end-to-end
Troubleshoot, debug, and resolve software defects and production issues
Support CI/CD pipelines, automated testing, and DevOps practices to enable efficient delivery
Ensure applications meet enterprise security, compliance, and data governance requirements
Continuously learn and apply new technologies, tools, and engineering patterns
Contribute to operational readiness, including monitoring, logging, and supportability of applications

You'll be rewarded and recognized for your performance in an environment that will challenge you and give you clear direction on what it takes to succeed in your role as well as provide development for other roles you may be interested in.

Required Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or a related technical field
3 + years of experience in software development (or equivalent internships, co-ops, or academic projects)
1+ years of experience in one or more programming languages such as Java, Python, C#, JavaScript, or TypeScript

Preferred Qualifications:

Experience with modern software development practices, including object-oriented design, APIs, and version control (Git)
Experience working with databases (SQL and/or NoSQL)
Familiarity with SDLC methodologies (Agile/Scrum preferred)
Understanding of data structures, algorithms, and system design fundamentals
Exposure to cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud Platform) is a plus
Strong problem-solving skills and ability to debug complex issues
Strong written and verbal communication skills
Ability to collaborate effectively in a cross-functional team environment

*All Telecommuters will be required to adhere to UnitedHealth Group's Telecommuter Policy.
