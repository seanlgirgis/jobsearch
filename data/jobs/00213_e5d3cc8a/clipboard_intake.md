Summary
Lead Data Platform Engineer to support a large-scale cloud modernization initiative focused on migrating and optimizing enterprise big data platforms within AWS environments.

This role will be part of a Multi-Cloud Environment (Alibaba Cloud & AWS China) and focus on cloud infrastructure automation, big data platform engineering, production support, and modernization of enterprise data ecosystems. The ideal candidate will bring strong hands-on AWS experience combined with infrastructure automation and big data platform expertise.

*This role is Fully Remote*

Required Experience

Strong experience supporting production cloud environments
Hands-on AWS experience (EMR, Redshift, S3, IAM)
Experience with Terraform and/or Ansible
Strong scripting and automation experience (Python, Bash)
Experience supporting big data platforms and cloud-based data lakes
Experience with CI/CD pipelines and deployment automation
Experience with Spark and/or Informatica environments
Ability to support and troubleshoot large-scale production systems
Experience mentoring engineers or leading technical initiatives
Preferred Experience

Alibaba Cloud experience
Experience working within multi-account AWS environments
Public cloud certifications
Experience with enterprise cloud migration initiatives
Bilingual Chinese/English is a plus
