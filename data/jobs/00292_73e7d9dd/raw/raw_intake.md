Senior Data Engineer (AWS / Databricks)

Overview
We are partnering with a growing organization that is building out a modern, cloud-based data platform. They are looking for a Senior Data Engineer to play a key role in designing and scaling data solutions, with a strong focus on AWS and Databricks.

This role offers the opportunity to work in an evolving environment, contribute to architectural decisions, and help drive the adoption of modern data engineering practices.

Responsibilities
Design, build, and optimize scalable data pipelines in AWS
Work with Databricks to support data processing and analytics use cases
Contribute to the development of a modern data architecture (data lake/lakehouse)
Integrate data from multiple sources and support new data initiatives
Improve performance, reliability, and efficiency of data workflows
Collaborate with analytics, business, and technical teams to deliver data solutions
Help establish best practices around data engineering, deployment, and data quality

Qualifications
5+ years of experience in data engineering or related roles
Strong experience with AWS data services (S3, Redshift, Athena, or similar)
Experience working with Databricks 
Strong SQL and Python skills
Experience building and maintaining data pipelines and data models
Understanding of modern data architecture concepts (data lakes, lakehouse, etc.)
Ability to work in a fast-paced, evolving environment

Nice to Have
Experience with streaming or real-time data technologies
Exposure to machine learning or data science workflows
Experience with data governance, cataloging, or data quality tools

Additional Information
On-site in Houston, TX
Opportunity to work on a growing data platform with modern tools
High-impact role with visibility across the organization
