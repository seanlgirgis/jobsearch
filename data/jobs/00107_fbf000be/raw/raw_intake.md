Bachelor s degree in Computer Science, IT, or a related field.
Minimum 5 years of experience as a Data Engineer or Data Analyst.

Strong experience in dimensional data modeling (star and snowflake schemas).
Experience with Power BI, DAX, Tableau, or Python for reporting and visualization.
