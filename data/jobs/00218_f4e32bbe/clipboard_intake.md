Role: Engineer - Data Analyst

Location: 100% Remote

Mode: Temp to Hire


About the Opportunity

In this role, you will support both internal stakeholders and external clients by enabling secure report delivery, driving timely issue resolution, and maintaining a high standard of service that protects the customer experience.



In this role, you will:

Operational Ownership: Report Delivery

Support end-to-end report delivery for a variety of stakeholders, ensuring outputs are accurate, secure, and delivered on time

Maintain delivery calendars and integrated delivery plans, coordinating milestones, dependencies, and readiness checkpoints across cross-functional teams

Monitor workflow health and proactively identify risks, escalating issues as needed and contributing to mitigation plans

Validate data outputs and perform quality checks to ensure consistency and reliability of reporting deliverables


Analysis and Strategic Improvements

Review business and technical requirements and provide input into data design, reporting logic, and process improvements

Analyze data to identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities for operational improvement

Research, experiment with, and adopt new tools, technologies, and approaches to improve automation, scalability, and efficiency

Contribute to the development of more standardized, repeatable, and well-documented workflows


Support

Troubleshoot data and reporting issues, identifying root causes and supporting resolution

Fulfill ad hoc data requests and reporting needs for internal and external stakeholders

Partner with team members to ensure consistent communication and timely support


About You

You will have:

Bachelor s degree in Computer Science, Data Analytics, Engineering, or a related field, or equivalent experience

1 2 years of experience in data analysis, reporting, or software/data-related roles (internships and academic projects count)

Experience working with SQL and Python for data querying and analysis

Familiarity with data visualization tools such as Tableau

Experience working with large datasets and performing data validation or quality checks

Exposure to cloud platforms such as AWS

Understanding of data modeling, ETL processes, and data warehousing concepts

Exposure to version control (Git) and collaborative development workflows

Ability to communicate data insights clearly to both technical and non-technical audiences
