About Samsung Austin Semiconductor
Samsung is a world leader in advanced semiconductor technology. This Capacity Planning Engineer coordinates strategies between Production Teams and Fab Engineering teams to achieve monthly output requirements and delivery success.

Role and Responsibilities
- Perform capacity analysis on factory bottlenecks for long-term and short-term basis.
- Identify actions to reduce capacity gaps and drive actions to completion.
- Perform line analysis and supply chain forecasting; pinpoint constraints and improve line movement/balance.
- Perform bottleneck investigations using production plans, tool performance metrics, and capacity projects; summarize risk to leadership.
- Coordinate with manufacturing and engineering teams for throughput improvements; quantify impact.
- Work with engineering teams to improve daily operations and optimize tool performance.
- Perform equipment efficiency analysis and execute improvements.

Skills and Qualifications
- BS in Industrial/Electrical/Systems Engineering or related engineering/applied math.
- 5+ years in Capacity Planning, Data Analytics, Project Management, Production Strategy or Manufacturing Engineering.
- Proficient in Factory Physics methodologies and supply chain management.
- Strong analytical/problem-solving skills; data modeling and forecasting techniques.
- SQL, Python, or scripting for analytics/automation preferred.
