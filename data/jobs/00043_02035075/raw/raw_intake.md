Staff Software Engineer (AI) – Forward Engineering

Build the future of AI‑powered systems at Thomson Reuters

Join our Forward Engineering team and help reinvent how Thomson Reuters operates. As a Staff Software Engineer (AI), you'll architect and prototype AI‑driven systems—multi‑agent workflows, advanced RAG pipelines, intelligent automation—and work directly with business and product leaders to turn real problems into transformative, AI‑first solutions.

This is a hands‑on, high‑influence role for an engineer who thrives at the intersection of systems design, applied AI, and cross‑functional partnership.

About The Role

Build enterprise‑grade AI systems — including domain‑specific agent frameworks and advanced RAG architectures.
Drive AI innovation by leading proofs of concept, evaluating emerging techniques, and translating research into deployable solutions.
Own architecture — converting business needs into scalable designs, defining patterns, and guiding engineering best practices (MLOps / LLMOps).
Partner across TR — embedding with business units, SMEs, and product teams to co‑create AI solutions and accelerate adoption.
Scale prototypes into production and help shape engineering standards for TR’s AI‑first transformation.

About You

8+ years in software engineering, with 4+ years leading AI/ML system development.
Deep expertise in Python and modern ML/AI frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, JAX).
Strong foundations in machine learning, distributed systems, and scalable architectures.
Demonstrated ownership of AI lifecycle: deployment, monitoring, model management, evaluation.
Experience with AWS/Azure/GCP and infrastructure tooling (Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform).
Proven ability to integrate AI into production systems using APIs, pipelines, and cloud‑native patterns.
Experience designing and validating agentic systems, including evaluation frameworks for non‑deterministic model behavior.

AI‑Native Engineering Expectations

Comfortable using AI coding tools (Copilot, Cursor, Cline, Claude Code) to quickly understand codebases, generate solutions, and accelerate development.
Able to work with AI agents to scale output—parallelizing workstreams, speeding code review, and rapidly iterating based on feedback.
Track record of rapid prototyping, moving from idea → proof of concept at startup speed.

Preferred

Experience leading teams that build internal AI prototypes or agent‑driven systems.
Strong knowledge of vector DBs, embeddings, semantic search, or IR systems.
History of launching and scaling AI solutions inside enterprise environments.
Familiarity with AI governance, model‑risk management, or regulated workflows.
Publications, patents, or speaking experience in AI/ML.
