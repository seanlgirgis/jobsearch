Our client, a leading organization in the technology and data industry, is seeking a Data Engineer to join their team.

As a Data Engineer, you will be part of the Data Engineering Department supporting the Data Team. The ideal candidate will have strong problem-solving skills, excellent communication abilities, and a proactive mindset, which will align successfully in the organization.

Job Title: Data Engineer

Location: Dallas, TX (onsite 3-4 days)

Pay Range: $75-93/hour based on experience

* No 3rd Parties/Corp-to-Corp Allowed ****

What's the Job?

Independently design, build, and launch new data extraction, transformation, and loading processes in production.

Identify the data needed for business problems and implement logging to ensure data availability, working with data infrastructure to troubleshoot issues and resolve them.

Build data expertise and leverage data controls to ensure privacy, security, compliance, data quality, and operational efficiency.

Design, build, and launch new data models to support evolving business needs.

Support existing processes running in production and implement optimized solutions with limited guidance, working with large-scale data environments.

What's Needed?

Minimum of 5 years of relevant experience in data engineering or similar roles.

Technical expertise with data models, data mining, and segmentation techniques.

Proficiency in Python, Scala, SQL, and scripting languages.

Experience with Big Data platforms such as Databricks, EMR, Hadoop.

Hands-on experience with SQL database design, ETL tools, and cloud environments like AWS, GCP, or Azure.

What's in it for me?

Opportunity to work with cutting-edge big data technologies and large-scale data environments.

Engagement in meaningful projects that impact business operations and decision-making.

Collaborative and innovative work environment supporting professional growth.

Chance to develop expertise in cloud platforms and data engineering tools.

Competitive compensation aligned with your experience and skills.
