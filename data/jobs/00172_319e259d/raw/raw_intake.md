Sr. Data Engineer: Cloud & Executive Insights


W2 Contract


Salary Range: $114,400 - $135,200 per year


Location: Cupertino, CA - Remote Role


Job Summary:


We are seeking a very senior Data Engineer with deep SQL expertise who can start quickly and deliver impact immediately. This role is highly execution-focused and requires end-to-end ownership of data pipelines, analytics, and business reporting used directly by leadership.


Duties and Responsibilities:



Experience working directly with and supporting Sr. Leadership

Design, build, and maintain end-to-end data pipelines integrating multiple data sources.

Develop and own business metrics, dashboards, and reporting consumed by senior leadership.

Perform data cleaning, validation, and transformation to ensure high data quality and trust.

Partner closely with product, analytics, and business stakeholders to translate requirements into scalable data solutions

Optimize SQL queries and data models for performance, reliability, and cost.

Take ownership of data delivery with minimal oversight and tight timelines.


Requirements and Qualifications:



Expert-level SQL (non-negotiable)

Complex joins, window functions, performance tuning, and large datasets

Strong end-to-end data engineering experience across the full data lifecycle

Proficiency in Python (preferred) or Java

Experience with Kubernetes and Kueue

Hands-on experience with Snowflake and modern analytics stacks

Proven ability to move fast, work independently, and deliver results under pressure

Experience building pipelines and analytics that directly support executive decision-making


Preferred Qualifications:



Experience with distributed data systems (e.g., Trino)

Familiarity with Lakehouse architectures

Prior experience building dashboards and analytics for executive audiences

Exposure to large-scale or high-growth data environments
