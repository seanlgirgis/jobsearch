Role Overview

Could you be our next Cloud Data Engineer? Globe Life is looking for a Cloud Data Engineer to join the team!

In this role, you will be responsible for supporting Cloud Data Management and Advanced Analytics platforms. In this role, an employee will work with data services, data analysts, and the data scientist team(s) to help the organization build out secure, scalable, fault-tolerant, and high-performing cloud-based architecture to enable and sustain data-driven decisions.

This is a hybrid position located in McKinney, Texas (WFH Monday & Friday, In Office Tuesday-Thursday).

What You Will Do
- Design, implement, and support various database services of our Cloud Platform.
- Provide technological guidance on Data Lake and Enterprise Data Warehouse design, development, implementation, and monitoring.
- Understand, design, and implement Data Security around cloud infrastructures.
- Provide support and guidance to Data Services and other application development teams on various AWS Database Products.
- Collaborate with leadership on process improvements and strategic initiatives for the Cloud Platform.

What You Can Bring
- 5+ years of IT experience.
- 3+ years of Database Engineering, primarily AWS Redshift, RDS/Aurora, DynamoDB, DMS, Glue.
- Proven experience in data pipelines and database applications.
- Strong coding/scripting in Python, PowerShell, or similar.
- Experience with Big Data technologies (Hadoop, Spark, Presto, Hive) is a plus.
- Financial Services domain is a plus.
- AWS Certifications are a plus.
- Experience with AWS DMS and migrating SQL Server/Oracle on-prem to AWS (Aurora/Redshift).
- Working knowledge of AWS services including S3, Lambda, Batch, Glue, Athena, EC2, EBS, CloudWatch, CloudTrail, ECS, ECR, EMR, IAM, SNS.
- ETL experience, preferably Informatica.
- Data lake / warehouse architecture in cloud.
- Experience with CloudFormation templates.
- S3 lifecycle management.
- IAM, roles, policies, federated access, KMS/encryption.
- Database performance testing and capacity planning.
- SDLC and agile.
- Excellent communication; independent and team work.
