Data Engineer - Database Support
Job at a Glance
Title: Data Engineer - Database Support
Location: Dallas, TX
Contract: W2 only, 1+ year
Pay: $60/hour + optional medical, dental, vision, 401(k) match
Overview
This position involves coordinating the collection, normalization, and analysis of datasets across multiple data platforms. The role includes assisting in designing and building data service infrastructure, ensuring functionality in data systems, and developing scalable and maintainable data solutions. The candidate will own significant components of the database platform and lead technical workstreams to ensure operational excellence.

Key Responsibilities
Coordinate the collection, normalization and analysis of datasets across multiple data platforms.
Assist in designing and building data service infrastructure across multiple data platforms.
Work with analytics teams to ensure functionality in data systems.
Participate in the development of and implementation of data solutions for multiple applications to ensure scalability and maintainability.
Design and implement process improvements including automation of processes and redesign of data infrastructure.
Own significant components of the database platform (specific schemas/domains, key performance areas, or HA/DR operational ownership) with minimal guidance.
Lead technical workstreams (e.g., partitioning rollout, performance remediation, operational readiness) and drive them to closure.
Improve runbooks/automation; contribute to production incident response and prevention.
Required Skills
Strong hands-on experience designing and operating high-volume, enterprise OLTP databases supporting mission-critical transactions.
Advanced SQL and performance tuning experience.
MySQL and Oracle experience with operational best practices: backup/restore validation, patch planning, monitoring/alerting, incident response, and runbooks.
Proven ability to collaborate across engineering, operations, and security with clear, structured communication.
Experience supporting high-volume transaction databases used for mission-critical business transactions.
Required Education
No Education Requirements
Preferred Skills
Data Guard experience (HA/DR, role transitions, validation of primary/standby states).
GoldenGate experience (replication design/operations).
Understanding of enterprise MySQL deployment/replication patterns (e.g., primary/replica cross-site patterns).
Exadata experience.
