About Us

We are a global climate technologies company engineered for sustainability. We create sustainable and efficient residential, commercial and industrial spaces through HVACR technologies. We protect temperature-sensitive goods throughout the cold chain. And we bring comfort to people globally. Best-in-class engineering, design and manufacturing combined with category-leading brands in compression, controls, software and monitoring solutions result in next-generation climate technology that is built for the needs of the world ahead.

Whether you are a professional looking for a career change, an undergraduate student exploring your first opportunity, or recent graduate with an advanced degree, we have opportunities that will allow you to innovate, be challenged and make an impact. Join our team and start your journey today!

Key Responsibilities

Description of duties & activities
Data analytics and reporting
Understand the business requirements and translate them into data needs and matrices
Work on data modeling (ETL) using snowflake or relevant database
Maintain existing dataset, dashboards in Snowflake, Power BI, Sigma or similar tools.
Build dashboards, KPI and performance reports to support decision making
Validate data accuracy and work with teams to address quality issues.
Business Analysis & Project Support
Work with stakeholders to gather and refine business requirements.
Prepare business requirement document and functional specifications.
Support and track project scope, ensure development aligned with business objectives
Coordinate with functional & technical teams during design and delivery
Prepare and test cases, execute testing, and obtain stakeholders sign off.
Support rollout of new features or process changes across teams

3. Automation
Develop automated workflows to reduce manual efforts and improve process efficiency.
Use automation tools or scripting to streamline repetitive tasks across business functions.
Identify opportunities for process optimization and implement scalable solutions.
Monitor and maintain automation outputs to ensure reliability.

4. Integration & Quality Testing

Support integration planning and ensure data and application components works effectively
Develop and maintain the test cases covering various business scenarios and workflows.
Conduct end-to-end integration testing to ensure reliability, compatibility, and optimal performance in real-world conditions.
