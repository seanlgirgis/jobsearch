About the role:

As a Senior Data Engineer, you will contribute to a variety of areas including:
The development of our overall data platform
Supporting internal and external customers with data related technical work, to include planning, documentation, and coding
Interacting directly with customers

About You

You're a good fit for the role of Senior Data Engineer if you meet the following criteria:
Have Bachelor of Science degree in a technical area (relevant experience will be considered in lieu of a degree).
7 years of data engineering experience
Proficiency in SQL and Snowflake Data Cloud Platform, including complex queries and performance optimization within the Snowflake environment
Experience with:
Data Loading, Data Transformation, Data Extraction, Sharing and Replication
Security features, including access control, data masking and encryption
Data modeling across disparate data sources with a variety of formats and structures
Ontology development at a warehouse level
Proficiency in at least one programming language (Python, Java, etc) for data processing and ETL scripting
Passionate, smart, and articulate technologist
Able to work independently based on high level instructions
Possess a strong personal work ethic
Desire to collaborate with a distributed team
Ability to obtain and maintain a U.S. National Security Clearance
Able to support and teach others in areas of mastery

Additional Desired Skills
Experience with formal SDLC (Software Development Life Cycle) procedures
Experience with version control software and methodology, preferably Github
