Nobilis Group is hiring a Lead Data Engineer for a new leadership role on the data team. The role is onsite in Irving, Texas and is contract-to-hire. The client needs someone who can lead and mentor the data team from day one while staying hands-on with SQL, Azure Data Factory, enterprise data pipelines, data migrations, API integrations, Power BI reporting support, and data platform architecture.

Company Context:
Nobilis Group began as Dent Zone in 1991 and provides vehicle protection, repair, fulfillment, dispatch repair networks, paintless dent repair services, protective coatings, and catastrophic repair support through PDR LINX. The company supports insurance partners and operational teams through a growing data ecosystem involving financial, sales, production, and operational reporting.

Role Overview:
The Lead Data Engineer will shape and scale the company’s data infrastructure. The role requires hands-on engineering, data leadership, strong SQL, Azure Data Factory, API-based integrations, ETL processes, database design, data quality, governance, and clear communication with analysts, report developers, stakeholders, and multiple departments.

The hiring manager emphasized that this is more than an individual contributor role. The person must be able to mentor the team, lead technical direction, and contribute immediately. Current team size is five. The team supports normal reporting through Power BI, mostly financial and sales production reports. The team uses the Power BI App for report distribution and is moving away from SSRS toward Power BI.
