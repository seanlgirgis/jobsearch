Role Overview: Data Integration Developer


Location: San Antonio, TX (On-site)


Duration: 7-Month Project


Are you ready to transform complex data into actionable business intelligence? We are looking for a skilled Data Integration Developer to join our team in San Antonio for a high-impact, 7-month project. In this role, you will bridge the gap between disparate datasets-ranging from weather and grid infrastructure to market operations-to build automated solutions that predict and respond to real-time business needs.


What You'll Do


Architect & Automate: Design, develop, and test robust ETL/Data Integration solutions using Informatica, Mulesoft, and Oracle PL/SQL.


Innovate with Big Data: Leverage technologies like Apache Spark, NiFi, Kafka, and Python to manage large-scale data flows and secure enterprise-wide data assets.


Solve Complex Problems: Troubleshooting data quality issues, performing bug fixes, and tuning performance to ensure high-speed, high-quality application delivery.


Modernize Operations: Utilize DevOps tools for continuous integration and monitoring while working within a collaborative, fast-paced environment.
