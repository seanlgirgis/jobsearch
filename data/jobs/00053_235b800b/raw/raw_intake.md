Job Summary

We are seeking a Junior Data Engineer to support the design, development, and optimization of data pipelines and data models within an AWS-based environment. This role will work closely with cross-functional teams to build scalable data solutions that support business intelligence, analytics, and data-driven decision-making across the organization.

This is an excellent opportunity for someone with strong foundational experience in data engineering who is looking to grow in a collaborative, enterprise environment.



What You’ll Do

Collaborate with business and technical teams to translate data and reporting needs into scalable data solutions
Design, build, and maintain data pipelines (batch and real-time) across multiple data sources
Develop and maintain data models to support analytics and reporting initiatives
Support integration of internal systems, APIs, and external data sources
Implement data quality checks, monitoring, and alerting for pipeline performance and failures
Troubleshoot data issues and perform root cause analysis with clear documentation
Optimize database performance through indexing, query tuning, and efficient schema design
Maintain documentation for data models, pipelines, and processes
Support migration and decommissioning of legacy data pipelines


What You Bring

2–3 years of experience in data engineering or related roles
Strong SQL skills, including query optimization and database design
Experience with Python for data processing and automation
Experience designing and managing data pipelines and ETL processes
Understanding of data modeling concepts (dimensional modeling, schema design)
Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
Ability to communicate effectively with both technical and non-technical stakeholders


Nice to Have

Experience with Databricks, Snowflake, Microsoft Fabric, or similar platforms
Experience working in AWS environments
Exposure to SAP or enterprise ERP systems
Experience with Terraform or infrastructure-as-code tools
