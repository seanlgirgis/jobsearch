RESPONSIBILITIES:
Kforce's client in Saint Louis, MO is looking for a Senior Data Engineer to come aboard a Direct Hire Opportunity.

Responsibilities:
* Works autonomously to define and create ETL/ELT (Extract, Transform, Load/Extract, Load, Transform) resources
* The Senior Data Engineer may work directly with subject matter experts to define or troubleshoot data migration issues
* Works with external partners to ensure the health of data extracts
* Possesses an understanding of PHI/PII (Protected Health Information/Personally Identifiable Information) principles and how they apply to data engineering
* Collaborates with peers, team and functional leaders to categorize and segregate data
* As a Senior Data Engineer, you will provide guidance and training to less experienced staff
* Develops templates and standards for ETL/ELT (Extract, Transform, Load/Extract, Load, Transform) development

REQUIREMENTS:
* To be considered for this position, candidates must have experience in a similar role, or they must possess significant knowledge, experience, and abilities to successfully perform the responsibilities listed
* Relevant education and/or training will be considered a plus
