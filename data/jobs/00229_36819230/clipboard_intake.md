Job Description


As a Data Engineer II, you will provide expertise in data engineering tools and platforms within a defined domain. You will identify development and support needs, take on design responsibilities for data pipelines and transformations, and support operational readiness. This role facilitates collaboration across teams to ensure data solutions align with business goals and downstream consumers.


Responsibilities

Design, develop, and maintain robust, scalable, and secure data pipelines using Azure Data Factory.
Build and maintain curated data models in SQL Server to support analytics, reporting, and operational workloads.
Develop and optimize complex SQL queries, views, and stored procedures for performance and reliability.
Implement data ingestion, transformation, and loading patterns across multiple source systems.
Collaborate with crossfunctional teams to translate business requirements into technical data solutions.
Write clean, efficient, welldocumented, and maintainable pipeline and transformation code.
Participate in code reviews and contribute to shared data engineering standards and best practices.
Ensure data pipeline performance, scalability, reliability, and security, including appropriate access controls.
Design and maintain data quality, pipeline health, and monitoring metrics; support alerting and operational runbooks.
Develop and maintain technical documentation in compliance with IT policies and standards.
Troubleshoot and resolve complex data and pipeline issues; participate in root cause analysis.
Mentor and guide junior engineers through reviews and knowledge sharing.

Requirements

Bachelor s degree in computer science, engineering, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience)
5+ years of experience in data engineering or related roles
Strong expertise in SQL Server, including performance tuning and data modeling
Handson experience building and supporting pipelines with Azure Data Factory
Experience working with .NET C# and Azure
Solid experience with data migration and transformation
Experience with source control in Git and Azure DevOps
Familiarity with Agile development methodologies (Scrum, Kanban)
Strong problemsolving skills, attention to detail, and effective communication skills

Nice to Haves

Experience with Azure data platform components Azure SQL and Azure Storage
Experience implementing data quality, governance, or lineage practices
Knowledge of Financial Services or Banking data domains
Prior experience leading data initiatives or mentoring engineers
