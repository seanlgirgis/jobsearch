Description

We are looking for an experienced Data Engineer to join our team in New York, New York. In this role, you will design, build, and maintain data infrastructure to support business intelligence and analytics needs. The ideal candidate will have a strong technical background, a passion for working with complex datasets, and expertise in cloud-based data platforms.

Responsibilities:
Develop, implement, and optimize ETL pipelines to ensure efficient data processing and integration.
Design and maintain scalable data solutions, including data warehouses and data lakes.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify data requirements and deliver actionable insights.
Utilize Snowflake, AWS, and other cloud-based platforms to manage data infrastructure and ensure performance optimization.
Leverage Python and SQL to build robust data workflows and automate processes.
Employ orchestration tools like Airflow and dbt to streamline data operations.
Support data analytics and visualization efforts by enabling the creation of impactful dashboards using tools such as Tableau.
Work with marketing and product data sources, including platforms like Google Analytics, to extract and integrate valuable insights.
Implement CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices to enhance data engineering processes.
Ensure data security and compliance across all systems and tools.

Requirements

At least 7 years of experience in data engineering or analytics-related roles.
Advanced proficiency in Python programming and SQL query development.
Strong hands-on experience with Snowflake or BigQuery, including expertise in data modeling and optimization.
Familiarity with orchestration tools such as Airflow and data integration frameworks like dbt.
Proven experience working with AWS services, including S3, Lambda, and Batch.
Knowledge of data visualization tools, particularly Tableau, and ability to support dashboard development.
Experience with CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices for data engineering.
Understanding of marketing and product data sources, including Google Analytics and similar platforms.
