**Job Description**

Data Engineer to design, build, and maintain scalable data pipelines and infrastructure that support analytics, reporting, and data-driven decision making. This role will work closely with data analysts, data scientists, and software engineering teams to ensure reliable and high-quality data solutions.

*Essential Duties and Responsibilities:*  

**Architecture & Design**

-   Design and own our end-to-end data platform architecture on Databricks and Apache Spark
-   Define standards for data modeling, pipeline design, and data quality across the organization
-   Evaluate and introduce new tools and frameworks as the team and company scales
-   Drive decisions on medallion architecture, Delta Lake structure, and cluster optimization

**Engineering & Delivery**

-   Build and maintain robust, scalable ELT/ETL pipelines using Spark and Databricks workflows
-   Develop reusable frameworks, libraries, and templates that accelerate team productivity
-   Own reliability and observability of our data pipelines --- monitoring, alerting, and incident response
-   Write clean, well-documented, testable code and hold the bar for engineering quality

**Analytics & Enablement**

-   Partner with analytics and business stakeholders to translate complex needs into sound data models
-   Build self-serve data assets that reduce bottlenecks and empower non-engineering users
-   Support ad-hoc data investigations and exploratory analysis as needed

**Team Leadership & Mentorship**

-   Act as a technical mentor and go-to resource for junior and mid-level engineers on the team
-   Lead design reviews, code reviews, and architectural discussions
-   Help define hiring standards and contribute to interviewing and onboarding as the team grows
-   Serve as a thought partner to leadership on data strategy and platform roadmap

**Required Qualifications**

-   7+ years of data engineering experience, with 2+ years operating at a senior or staff level
-   Deep expertise with Apache Spark --- performance tuning, optimization, and production troubleshooting
-   Hands-on experience with Databricks including Unity Catalog, Delta Live Tables, and Databricks Workflows
-   Strong SQL skills and experience with dimensional modeling, data vault, or medallion architecture
-   Experience designing and maintaining production-grade data pipelines with high reliability standards
-   Comfortable operating as a generalist --- you can move between infrastructure, pipelines, and analytics layers
-   Strong communication skills; able to explain technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders

**Preferred Qualifications**

-   Experience with Python-based orchestration tools (Airflow, Prefect, or similar)
-   Familiarity with dbt for transformation layers
-   Cloud platform experience (AWS), cloud-native storage and compute
-   Experience building with small teams at a growth-stage company
-   Background in analytics engineering or data architecture in addition to core engineering
-   Knowledge of data governance and security best practices
-   Experience in financial services or highly regulated environments
