job summary:


We are seeking Data Engineers (contract) to join our talented Data Engineering team. In this role, you will help design and build scalable, high-performance data pipelines that ingest and transform over 30TB of data daily, ensuring reliability, efficiency, and data integrity across our platform.




location: Telecommute

job type: Contract

salary: $165 - 170 per hour

work hours: 9am to 5pm

education: Bachelors



responsibilities:


You will take on the following responsibilities:


? Work closely with business partners across Two Sigma to understand their data


ingestion and/or data transformation needs


? Work with engineers, researchers, and portfolio managers across the organization to


onboard interesting new datasets and to productionalize new data pipelines


? Build stable, productionalized data pipelines to support Two Sigma's trading activities


? Extend, improve, and keep up-to-date, our data ingestion and transformation framework


as needed



qualifications:


You should possess the following qualifications:


? You have at least a bachelor's degree in a technical field


? You have at least 3 years of full-time work experience as a Data Engineer or in a similar


role


? You have experience developing data workflows and a good operational understanding


of data operations, including processing, storage, quality and management


? You have at least 3 years experience in building ETL pipelines


? You have at least 3 years experience with Python programming
