## Why This Pivot Works

Manufacturing capacity planning is the **origin** of the discipline. Factory Physics, Little's Law, takt time, OEE — all invented for factories. IT borrowed them. You are going from the application back to the source. That is not a stretch — it is the natural direction.

If you believe that framing, it comes through. If you are apologizing for it, it does not.
