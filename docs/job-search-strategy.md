# Job Search Strategy
*Where to look and what to look for beyond LinkedIn.*

Last updated: 2026-03-11

---

## Target Roles (Expanded)

Do not limit to Senior Data Engineer. Cast wider:

| Role Title | Why It Fits | Speed |
|---|---|---|
| Data Engineer | Core match, less gatekeeping | Fast |
| Senior Data Engineer | Core match, full match | Medium |
| Data Platform Engineer | Same work, different title | Fast |
| Analytics Engineer | SQL/dbt/pipeline focus | Fast |
| ETL Developer / ETL Engineer | Older title, less competition | Fast |
| Pipeline Engineer | Exact what you do | Fast |
| Performance Engineer | 20 years direct experience | Fast |
| Capacity Engineer | HorizonScale is your proof | Fast |
| Capacity Planner | Citi role directly maps | Fast |
| Infrastructure Data Engineer | Telemetry + pipelines | Medium |
| Site Reliability Engineer (Data) | Observability + pipelines | Medium |
| APM Engineer | CA APM / Dynatrace SME | Fast |
| Observability Engineer | AppDynamics + Dynatrace | Fast |

**Rule:** If the job description mentions Python, SQL, pipelines, or monitoring — read it. Don't disqualify yourself by title alone.

## Recruiter Outreach (High ROI)

Recruiters are your fastest path. They get paid when you get placed — they want to help.

**How to find them on LinkedIn:**
Search: `recruiter "data engineer" Dallas` or `technical recruiter fintech Texas`

**Message template:**
> "Hi [Name] — I'm a Senior Data Engineer with 20 years of experience (Python, PySpark, AWS, Airflow, SQL) currently open to new opportunities in the DFW area. Open to Data Engineer, Performance Engineer, or Capacity roles. Would love to connect if you're working anything relevant."

**Target recruiting firms for tech in DFW:**
- Robert Half Technology
- TEKsystems
- Apex Group
- Insight Global
- Randstad Technologies
- Motion Recruitment
- Hired.com (tech-specific, they pitch you to companies)
