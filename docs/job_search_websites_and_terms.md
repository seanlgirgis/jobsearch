## 4) Copy/Paste Boolean Search Strings

## 4.1 Senior Data Engineer (General)
- "Senior Data Engineer" AND (Python AND SQL) AND (PySpark OR Spark) AND (AWS OR "Amazon Redshift" OR Athena) AND (Airflow OR ETL OR ELT)
- ("Staff Data Engineer" OR "Lead Data Engineer") AND ("data platform" OR "data pipelines") AND (AWS OR GCP OR Azure)

## 4.2 Capacity Planning Focus
- ("Capacity Planning Engineer" OR "Capacity Engineer") AND (forecasting OR "time-series" OR Prophet OR "scikit-learn") AND (infrastructure OR platform)
- ("Senior Data Engineer" AND "capacity planning") AND (Python OR SQL) AND (monitoring OR observability)
- ("Infrastructure Capacity" OR "Capacity Forecasting") AND ("resource optimization" OR right-sizing OR utilization)

## 4.3 Performance Monitoring / APM Focus
- ("Performance Engineer" OR "APM Engineer" OR "Observability Engineer") AND (Dynatrace OR AppDynamics OR "CA APM" OR Introscope OR "BMC TrueSight")
- ("Senior Performance Engineer") AND (latency OR throughput OR bottleneck) AND (monitoring OR observability)
- ("Application Performance") AND ("real user monitoring" OR synthetic OR "end-to-end")

## 4.4 Cloud Data + Forecasting Hybrid
- ("Data Platform Engineer" OR "Cloud Data Engineer") AND (AWS AND (Glue OR Athena OR Redshift OR S3)) AND (forecasting OR "capacity planning")
- ("Senior Data Engineer") AND ("serverless" OR lakehouse) AND (PySpark OR Spark) AND (forecasting OR performance)

## 4.5 AI + Data Engineering Hybrid
- ("AI Data Engineer" OR "GenAI Data Engineer" OR "LLM Engineer") AND (RAG OR "Text-to-SQL" OR embeddings) AND (Python OR SQL)
- ("Data Engineer") AND (Bedrock OR "LLM" OR "agentic") AND (pipeline OR orchestration)
