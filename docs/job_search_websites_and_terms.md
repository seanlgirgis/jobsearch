## 2.6 Copy/Paste Boolean Search Strings

### LinkedIn / Indeed style
- "Data Engineer" AND (Python OR PySpark) AND (AWS OR GCP OR Azure) AND (Airflow OR dbt)
- ("Senior Data Engineer" OR "Staff Data Engineer") AND (Snowflake OR BigQuery OR Redshift)
- "Analytics Engineer" AND dbt AND SQL AND (Snowflake OR BigQuery)
- ("Data Platform Engineer" OR "Big Data Engineer") AND (Spark OR Databricks) AND Kafka
- "Data Engineer" AND healthcare AND (HIPAA OR FHIR OR claims)
- "Data Engineer" AND fintech AND (risk OR payments OR fraud)

### Google X-ray style
- site:linkedin.com/jobs "data engineer" "remote" "United States"
- site:greenhouse.io "data engineer" "remote"
- site:lever.co "senior data engineer" "remote"
- site:boards.greenhouse.io "analytics engineer"

## 2.8 Fast Daily Search Routine
1. Run 3 saved searches on LinkedIn (Remote US, Chicago, Dallas).
2. Run the same 3 on Indeed.
3. Check Built In + Wellfound for startup roles.
4. Check 10 target company career pages directly.
5. Track matches and apply to top-fit roles first.
