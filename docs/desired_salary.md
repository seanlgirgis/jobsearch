# Salary Dictionary
*My go-to reference for every salary question during the job search.*

Last updated: 2026-03-11

---

## Default Answer (Use This Every Time)

> "Honestly, compensation is secondary to finding the right role and team. If there's a strong fit, I'm confident we can align on numbers."

**When to use:** Application forms, early recruiter screens, any time they ask before an interview.

---

## If They Push for a Number

> "I'm targeting $155,000 to $170,000 base, based on my 20 years of experience and the market for senior data engineering roles in this area. I'm open to discussing the full compensation package including bonus and equity."

---

## Hard Rules

- **Floor: $140K** — do not go below this no matter how desperate the moment feels
- Never give a single number — always a range
- If they push back on the range, pivot to total comp (bonus + equity + benefits)
- If a form forces a number, enter `150000` (your floor, not your target)
