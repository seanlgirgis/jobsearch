# Unique Test Job - Underwater Basket Weaver

## Overview
We are looking for a skilled Underwater Basket Weaver to join our team at AquaCraft Inc.

## Responsibilities
- Weave baskets while fully submerged in a tank.
- Ensure structural integrity of woven materials under high pressure.
- Collaborate with marine biologists to source sustainable reeds.

## Requirements
- 5+ years of experience in sub-aquatic crafts.
- Scuba certification required.
- Ability to hold breath for 3+ minutes is a plus.

## Location
Atlantis (Hybrid Remote)
